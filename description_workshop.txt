This mod aims to remove some slowdowns in the game UI that occur when the game lags due to their animation or interaction speed being tied to framerate

[b]Currently these include:[/b]
- The scroll speed of the crafting menu
- The acceleration and speed of crafting items in bulk
- The acceleration and speed of buying items in bulk or picking up items in bulk with Right click
